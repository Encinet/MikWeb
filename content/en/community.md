# Community Guide

## Getting Involved

1. **Say hi and join the conversation** - Talk in chat, share what you are building, and take part in community activities.
2. **Help other players** - Share building tips, answer questions, and contribute to public projects when you can.
3. **Show your work** - Credit the builder and original creator where appropriate, then invite other players to visit.

## Contact

### Players in Mainland China

- **QQ Group** - Join the QQ group to talk with other players

### Overseas Players

- **Application Form** - Apply through the form and wait for the email response

## Getting Your Build Featured

If you want your build to appear on the official server website, contact an admin and submit it for review.

**Requirements**

- The build must be original, or you must have permission from the original creator
- You need to provide the coordinates so the build can be verified
- You need to provide at least one screenshot

**Accepted submission types**

- Original builds
- Replicas or recreations, as long as the original creator is credited

Collaborative builds can be credited to multiple people together.
