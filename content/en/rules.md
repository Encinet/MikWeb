# Server Rules

## Server Nature

This is a creative casual server that allows any mods (including hacks), as long as you don't destroy buildings or others' creations.

## Building Guidelines

1. **Do not destroy others' buildings** or terrain beyond what's necessary for construction
2. **Builds must harmonize with surroundings**, avoid obvious discontinuities or abrupt structures, site selection must be reasonable
3. **Light redstone is allowed**, large-scale or server-lagging redstone devices are prohibited, minimize high-frequency operations
4. **Label the builder and original author** (if any) in a reasonable location within the building

> **Example**: You cannot dig holes in the wild, cut a mountain in half without smoothing it, etc.
